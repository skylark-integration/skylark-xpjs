Reborn XP - The Windows XP Simulator

Step back in time and experience the nostalgia of the classic Windows XP operating system, fully recreated in your web browser. This is an interactive and functional simulation, not just a video or a theme.

  • Explore a working desktop with a functional Start Menu, taskbar, and icons.
  • Customize your experience with the complete suite of authentic themes, including Luna, Royale, Zune, and Classic.
  • Use classic applications, listen to music in Windows Media Player, and play iconic games.
  • Create and save files to a persistent virtual file system that remembers your work between sessions.
